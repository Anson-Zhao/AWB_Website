PSCA: Areas “B" and “C" Mapping 21Apr16

Multi-image Orthogrammetry Maps

Trillium Learning’s A World Bridge™ current UAV research activities at Alaska Aerospace - Pacific Spaceport Complex Alaska (PSCA) in Kodiak, Alaska are part of the University of Alaska system’s FAA UAV research program. 

The following media were obtained from PSCA on April 21, 2016, and processed by Trillium Learning. 


Mission Parameters Area “C"

Unmanned Aircraft System: DJI Inspire 1 with X3 Camera. 

Area Mapped: 282 acres

Images for orthomosaic: 586, click here for .zip package of all images - 3.06 GB

Altitude: 350 ft  

Resolution: 4.5 cm/px
