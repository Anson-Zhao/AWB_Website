NASA CitySmart - United Nations Sustainable Development Goals for Sustainable Communities

Partners include: NASA Ames Research Center, United Nations (Sustainable Development Goals), Open GeoSpatial Consortium, International GeoSpatial Laboratories, Kodiak Electric Association, UMass at Amherst, Springfield-Oregon, Middletown-New York, and multiple international cities.

A World Bridge® projects focus on development of resource management tools for urban administrators. These open source tools are meant to promote greater collaboration between cities for sharing solutions needed by all. Geospatial data involving the UN Sustainable Development Goals for energy, food, water, air, etc., are visualized using NASA virtual globe technology.

The development of a Smart Cities Spatial Information Framework is based on these themes:

  • Smarter Cities are high-density generators of innovation
  • Geospatial information is a major enabler of Smart City technology benefits
  • Infrastructure management is vital to urban resilience
  • Open standards are needed for interoperability, efficiency, application innovation and cost effectiveness
